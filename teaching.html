I am extremely enthusiastic about teaching, which is an important component of my academic career. As an educator, I strive to engage, challenge, and inspire my students to think about their world in new ways and with a scientific perspective. My goal is to both teach my students the traditional materials found in a textbook, and provide them with important skills, such as scientific reasoning, critical thinking and problem solving, which are essential for any career they choose to pursue. I find it very rewarding and satisfying by teaching, advising students, and growing with my students.

The following comments are from my previous students on the final course evaluation forms. These words make me feel rewarded, and strongly encourage me to improve myself to be a better educator!
